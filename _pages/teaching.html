Teaching Assistant

I have fulfilled the duty of being a Teaching Assistant (TA) for junior year students for their core courses. As a TA I conducted weekly tutorial sessions for a batch ot 45 students to discuss problem sets and course content related doubts. Apart form this, as a TA I evaluated quizzes of students taking the course and provided course-work related guidance whenever needed.

So far I have been a TA for two courses:

  • Quantum Physics and Application (PH107)

  • Basics of Electricity and Magnetism (PH108)
